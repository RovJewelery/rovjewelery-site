Frequently asked

What materials do you use?+

Our pieces are offered in premium gold finishes and select solid 14K or 18K gold, with stone specifications clearly noted for each design.

How long does a custom order take?+

Most custom pieces take 4–8 weeks after design approval. Complex stonework or sourcing may require additional time.

Is shipping insured?+

Yes. Every RovJewelery order ships fully insured with signature confirmation and tracking.

How should I care for my jewelry?+

Store pieces separately, avoid harsh chemicals, and clean gently with a soft cloth. Contact us for professional servicing.
